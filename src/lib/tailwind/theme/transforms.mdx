# [Transforms](https://tailwindcss.com/docs/transform) <small className='text-xs'>[Jump to source](#source-code)</small>

The `transforms` property includes utilities for applying transformations to elements, such as rotation, scaling, skewing, and translation.

## `rotate`
Defines rotation utilities for elements.
<Source language="tsx" code={`rotate: ${JSON.stringify(component.rotate, null, 2)}`} />
- `0`: No rotation.
- `1`: 1 degree.
- `2`: 2 degrees.
- `3`: 3 degrees.
- `6`: 6 degrees.
- `12`: 12 degrees.
- `45`: 45 degrees.
- `90`: 90 degrees.
- `180`: 180 degrees.

## `scale`
Defines scale utilities for elements.
<Source language="tsx" code={`scale: ${JSON.stringify(component.scale, null, 2)}`} />
- `0`: 0 scale (invisible).
- `50`: 50% scale.
- `75`: 75% scale.
- `90`: 90% scale.
- `95`: 95% scale.
- `100`: 100% scale (original size).
- `105`: 105% scale.
- `110`: 110% scale.
- `125`: 125% scale.
- `150`: 150% scale.

## `skew`
Defines skew utilities for elements.
<Source language="tsx" code={`skew: ${JSON.stringify(component.skew, null, 2)}`} />
- `0`: No skew.
- `1`: 1 degree skew.
- `2`: 2 degrees skew.
- `3`: 3 degrees skew.
- `6`: 6 degrees skew.
- `12`: 12 degrees skew.

## `transformOrigin`
Defines the origin point for transformations.
<Source language="tsx" code={`transformOrigin: ${JSON.stringify(component.transformOrigin, null, 2)}`} />
- `center`: Center of the element.
- `top`: Top of the element.
- `top-right`: Top right corner of the element.
- `right`: Right of the element.
- `bottom-right`: Bottom right corner of the element.
- `bottom`: Bottom of the element.
- `bottom-left`: Bottom left corner of the element.
- `left`: Left of the element.
- `top-left`: Top left corner of the element.

## `translate`
Defines translation utilities based on theme spacing values.
<Source language="tsx" code={`translate: ${component.translate}`} />
- Includes values based on theme spacing, along with percentage values like `1/2`, `1/3`, `2/3`, `1/4`, `2/4`, `3/4`, and `full`.
